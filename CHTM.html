SUITABLE HOBBIES


If you're considering a program within the College of Hospitality and Tourism Management, fostering specific hobbies can enhance your educational journey. Engage in activities such as traveling, culinary arts, event planning, customer service, cultural exploration, language learning, and hotel management. These pursuits not only complement your academic studies but also develop skills essential for success in the hospitality and tourism industry.

JOBS AND PROFESSIONS


If you pursue a program within the College of Hospitality and Tourism Management, you open doors to various career paths. These may include roles such as hotel manager, event planner, travel agent, restaurant manager, tourism officer, cruise director, and hospitality consultant.
