COLLEGE DEPARTMENTS

College of Allied
Health Studies

A college dedicated to training the next generation of healthcare professionals. CAHS programs cover various allied
health disciplines, supported by experienced faculty and state-of-the-art facilities. Through hands-on learning
and clinical experiences, students gain
the skills and knowledge needed for successful careers in healthcare.

College of Business
and Accountancy

A college dedicated to shaping future leaders in accounting, finance, management, and business administration. CBA programs offer a comprehensive education, guided by experienced faculty, and enriched with hands-on learning opportunities.

College of Computer Studies

A college with expert faculty, and strong industry connections, CCS prepares students for success in technology careers. A college committed to constantly training the skills, enhancing the knowledge, and honing the expertise of its students. CCS continually aims to uphold its competencies to mold professional experts in the future.

College of Education, Arts and Sciences

A college that covers education, humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and fine arts. With dedicated faculty and engaging learning experiences, CEAS prepares students for a range of career paths.

College of Hospitality and Tourism Management

A college to prepare students for leadership roles in hospitality, tourism, and event management. Led by experienced faculty, CHTM blends theory with practical skills. Through internships and industry partnerships, students gain hands-on experience for successful careers in various sectors of the industry.
