Privacy Policy


GC Career Counseling is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of the personal information of the users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect:


Personal Information: We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and demographic information when you voluntarily submit it to us through forms on our website or during consultations.

Usage Data: We may also collect non-personal information about your interactions with our website, such as the pages visited, the time spent on each page, and the browser type, through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information:


Provide Services:We use the information collected to provide career counseling services, including assessing your needs, offering personalized guidance, and facilitating communication with counselors.

Improve Services:We may use aggregated usage data to analyze trends, enhance the functionality of our website, and improve the quality of our services.

Communication:We may use your contact information to send you updates, newsletters, or promotional materials related to our services, unless you opt out of receiving such communications.

Information Sharing and Disclosure:


Service Providers:We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as hosting providers, analytics companies, and communication platforms.

Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as court orders or subpoenas.

Consent:We may share your information with your consent or at your direction, such as when you request referrals to external resources or authorize us to share your information with potential employers or educational institutions.

Data Security:


We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Data Retention:


We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

You may request the deletion of your personal information by contacting us at any of our social media.

Your Rights:


You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also request information about the purposes of data processing or object to certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights or inquire about our data practices, please contact us at any of our social media.

Changes to this Privacy Policy:


We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will post the revised policy on our website with the updated "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of our website or services after the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of such changes.

Contact Us:


If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at any of our social media.



By using our website or services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
